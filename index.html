THE WORK WE DO

We work around existing issues surrounding the refugee cause with a goal of generating long term scalable solutiuons.

CORE CONCEPTS

  • OUR DRIVE
  • OUR WORK
  • OUR PLAN

Forced by war or persecution to flee their homelands, refugees face untold obstacles in gaining the capacity and access to fully operate in a society they are ressestled in.

By telling and sharing their stories we aim to to ease the adjustment process by cultivating self efficacy and providing refugees with valuable tools for advocating their interests , building cross-cultural community, informing and engaging the general public.

Through fostering partnerships we look forward to deepen ties with other international organizations as well as new organizational ties in the community.

OUR PURPOSE

In 2015 and 2016, UNHCR and WRC undertook the Global Refugee Youth Consultations (GRYC) to amplify voices of youth in decisions that affect them. The consultations were the beginning of a process that will continue to develop the leadership, capacity, and futures of refugee youth everywhere, according to UNHCR and WRC.
Through our program we do this in form of drawing , poetry ,exhibitions. This gives the refugees the opportunity and platform to express themselves in a unique and wholesome way. In a sense we believe we provide a guiding hand so that in different ways they can find their individual voices to speak where previously they could not.

The nature of using art workshops tends to make friendships blossom as participants share details about their histories and discover commonalities across their unique individual experiences.
